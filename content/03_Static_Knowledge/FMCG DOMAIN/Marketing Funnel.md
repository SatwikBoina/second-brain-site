**What is the purpose of having a marketing funnel?**
A framework that captures the journey of a consumer from being a stranger to a loyal customer of a brand.

In a way, it tries to improve two things.
- Growth in Sales or Margin
- Brand loyalty in Consumers.

The [[Media Channels]] are associated at every level of this funnel to employ the tactics pertaining to that particular level of the funnel.
