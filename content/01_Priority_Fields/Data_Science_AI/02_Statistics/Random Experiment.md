### 1️⃣ Definition

- The outcome is not fixed to the act when the experiment is done under ideal conditions.
- The concepts of probability is based on the Random Experiment because we are aware of the what shows up (sample space) but not which one at the moment.
---

###  2️⃣ Analogy / Intuition

An unbiased coin-toss may not land on the same side under ideal conditions.
